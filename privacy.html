Lalapass Privacy Policy

Our Privacy Philosophy

At Aila Squad, we believe the best way to protect your data is not to have it at all. [cite: 2026-02-04] This policy explains how we keep lalapass simple, secure, and entirely yours. [cite: 2025-12-18]

1. No Servers, Just Privacy

Lalapass is designed as a purely local application. [cite: 2026-02-18] We do not own or operate any servers to store your information. [cite: 2026-02-04] Every password and note you save stays exactly where it belongs: on your own device. [cite: 2026-02-18]

2. Military-Grade Security

  • Your data is guarded by AES-GCM 256-bit encryption, the gold standard for data protection. [cite: 2026-02-18]
  • Your master key is stored in your device's secure enclave. Because we don't have a server, even we couldn't access your data if we wanted to. [cite: 2026-02-04, 2026-02-18]
⚠️ Your Responsibility

Since your master key is never transmitted to us, we have no way to recover or reset it for you. Please keep your master key safe; if it is lost, your stored data will be permanently inaccessible. [cite: 2026-02-18]

3. Your Data, Your Control

We provide multiple ways to keep your data safe. You remain the sole owner and manager of your information.

  • Manual Backup & Restore: You can export your encrypted database to the Files app, or share it via email and messengers for safekeeping.
  • Encrypted Export: Every backup file is protected by the same AES-GCM 256-bit encryption. [cite: 2026-02-18] Without your master key, these files remain unreadable even if shared.
  • iCloud Sync: Optional cloud backups are handled exclusively within Apple’s secure environment.

4. Zero Data Collection

We don't collect your personal information, which means we have nothing to sell, trade, or share with anyone. It's that simple. [cite: 2026-02-04]
